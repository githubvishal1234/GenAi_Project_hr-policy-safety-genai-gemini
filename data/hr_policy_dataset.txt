Company Name: ABC Tech Solutions

1. Leave Policy
Employees are entitled to:
- 12 casual leaves per year
- 10 sick leaves per year
- 15 earned leaves per year

Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal
at least 2 days in advance, except in emergencies.

2. Working Hours Policy
- Standard working hours: 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM
- Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day
- Flexible working hours may be approved by managers

3. Remote Work Policy
- Employees may work remotely up to 2 days per week
- Approval from the reporting manager is required
- Remote work is subject to performance and role suitability

4. Code of Conduct
- Employees must maintain professional behavior
- Harassment or discrimination is strictly prohibited
- Company data must not be shared externally

5. Confidentiality Policy
- Employee salaries are strictly confidential
- Personal employee records are private
- Unauthorized access to internal data is prohibited

6. IT & Security Policy
- Company systems must be used responsibly
- Passwords must not be shared
- Any security incident must be reported to IT immediately

For sensitive queries, employees should contact the HR department directly.
